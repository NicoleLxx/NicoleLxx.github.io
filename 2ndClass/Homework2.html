HOW TO MAKE THE BEST PROTEIN SHAKE IN THE WORLD



Picture by The Fitchen



Ingredients
  • 1 cup of skimmed fat milk

  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder, can be adjusted by the personal daily protein requirment and taste preference

  • 1 cup of espresso coffee

  • 4 drops of vanilla extract

  • 1/2 cup of vanilla-flavoured ice cream

    •

Directions
  1. Cheesier taste than plain no-sugar coffee

  2. Easy to make, easy to grab and go.

  3. Enjoy your protein supplement as those blended coffee you normally get outside in the coffee shops, but in a healthier way!
