MY CITY


Brief Introduction

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times. At its centre stand the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations.

Population

  1. London has a diverse range of people and cultures, and more than 300 languages are spoken in the region.

  2. Its estimated mid-2016 municipal population was 8,787,892, the largest of any city in the European countries and accounting for 13.4% of the UK population.

  3. London’s urban area is the second most populous in Europe, after Paris, with 9,787,426 inhabitants at the 2011 census.

  4. The city’s metropolitan area is the most populous in the EU with 14,040,163 inhabitants in 2016.
